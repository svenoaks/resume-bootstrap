Music Speed Changer is a popular tool for musicians - it is capable of independently modifying the pich and tempo of audio in real time, and looping sections of the track for practice. File output of the modified audio is also possible.

The app involved developing a complete audio player with the Android SDK and NDK because low level access to the underlying byte stream was required to modify the audio.

The app makes extensive use of multithreading, as the processing of the audio happens off the UI thread, playback and the file saving operation occur on separate threads and the processing and file writing involved in the file saving operation are further divided on separate threads. This translates to superior performance and usability.

The app is more highly rated than it's peers, and growth of the user base continues to increase.

View the app in the Google Play Store

SoundTouch-Android is an Android wrapper for a C++ audio processing library.

As well as wrapping the low level functions with JNI, the library provides high level functionality for the Android platform, making it very easy for other developers to create apps involving pitch shifting or time stretching of audio files. The altered audio can be played back in real time or saved to an AAC encoded audio file.

View the library on GitHub

Music Flow is an app for music lovers that delivers up to the minute information on the music the user is listening to. When songs are played on many popular music players, they are listed in the "flow". The user can easily view the lyrics of the song, watch a video, get concert dates and information about the artist. The app can also identify music from the microphone, similar to Shazam.

The app relies heavily receiving JSON data from various web API's. Many of the layouts are quite complex and result in a high visual appeal.

View the app in the Google Play Store

Pdf Rotate and Merge solves a common problem with generating Pdf's. Many times Pdf's will be generated at the wrong orientation and free tools such as Adobe Reader are incapable of saving Pdf's with an adjusted rotation angle.

For many businesses, sending a Pdf to a client with the wrong orientation may appear unprofessional. Pdf Rotate and Merge solves this problem and is also capable of merging multiple Pdf's into a single Pdf file.
